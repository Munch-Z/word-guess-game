To play this game all you need to do is click 'Play Now' and then press the letters on your keyboard, that you think might be in the word. If you are able to guess the correct word before running out of guesses, you win! Additionally, you can change the word bank that your words are being selected from by clicking either "Perks" or "Equipment" at the top of the screen.
